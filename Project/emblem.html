EMBLEM TUTORIAL

Custom Assassin: Focused on enhancing physical attack, critical chance, and critical damage. Suitable for heroes with an assassin playstyle, emphasizing burst damage.

Custom Marksman: Geared towards marksmen, providing boosts to physical attack, critical chance, and critical damage. Enhances the damage output of heroes who rely on basic attacks.

Custom Fighter: Designed for fighters, offering bonuses to physical attack, attack speed, and physical penetration. Suitable for heroes that engage in sustained combat.

Custom Tank: Geared towards tanks, offering increased magic defense, HP, and cooldown reduction. Enhances survivability and resilience for heroes that act as frontline tanks.

Custom Mage: Tailored for mages, providing increased magic power, magic penetration, and cooldown reduction. Enhances the magical damage output and ability usage of mage heroes.

Custom Support: Intended for support heroes, providing bonuses to magic power, cooldown reduction, and movement speed. Enhances the utility and survivability of support-oriented heroes.
